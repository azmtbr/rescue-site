Why MyRescueSite?

Having worked with two animal rescues in my area for over five years, I've seen how little time and other resources are actually available to put towards creating a nice-looking, easy to interact with online presence. MyRescueSite will cater to the things that an animal rescue actually needs to worry about from day to day. Creating, updating, and removing the animal listings will be quick, easy, and will be able to be done from a mobile device unlike other template based services. Adoption forms will be easy for the user to complete, as well as simple for the rescue to access and print or save for their records. In short, each rescue will be able to update their very own rescue site with minimal time and effort, allowing them more time to help animals find homes.
